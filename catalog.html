Green Tea

Fresh and unoxidized, green tea is known for its grassy and slightly bitter flavor. Packed with antioxidants, it offers a delicate and soothing experience.

Black Tea

A fully oxidized tea with a robust, bold flavor. Black tea is perfect for those who enjoy a strong and invigorating cup.

Herbal Tea - Chamomile

Chamomile tea is a caffeine-free herbal infusion renowned for its calming and soothing properties. It has a sweet and floral taste.

Oolong Tea

Oolong tea falls between green and black tea, offering a rich and complex flavor profile. It's a perfect balance of both worlds.

Earl Grey Tea

Earl Grey is a classic black tea infused with the essence of bergamot orange. It boasts a fragrant, citrusy aroma and taste.

White Tea

White tea is made from young tea leaves and buds. It's known for its subtle, sweet, and delicate flavor.

Peppermint Tea

Peppermint tea is an invigorating herbal infusion that delivers a refreshing burst of minty flavor. It's caffeine-free and great for digestion.

Chai Tea

Chai tea is a spiced black tea popular in India. It's a flavorful blend of black tea, spices, and milk, known for its warmth and bold taste.

Jasmine Tea

Jasmine tea is a fragrant green tea infused with jasmine flowers. It offers a delightful floral aroma and a mild, soothing taste.

Rooibos Tea

Rooibos, or red bush tea, is a caffeine-free herbal tea from South Africa. It has a naturally sweet and earthy flavor.

Darjeeling Tea

Known as the "champagne of teas," Darjeeling tea is a black tea with a delicate and muscatel flavor, perfect for afternoon sipping.

Matcha tea

Matcha is a finely ground Japanese green tea, celebrated for its vibrant green color and rich, umami flavor. It's packed with antioxidants and provides a unique, energizing experience..
